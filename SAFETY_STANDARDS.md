# Safety Standards & Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01

At **Lovify**, we are dedicated to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all our users. To ensure a positive experience, we have established the following Safety Standards and Community Guidelines.

## 1. Zero Tolerance for Harassment & Bullying
We do not tolerate harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to:
*   Sending offensive or threatening messages.
*   Stalking or unwanted persistent contact.
*   Using hate speech or discriminatory language based on race, ethnicity, religion, disability, gender, age, or sexual orientation.

## 2. Prohibition of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
We have a strict **zero-tolerance policy** regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
*   Any user found sharing, soliciting, or promoting content depicting the sexual exploitation of minors will be **permanently banned** immediately.
*   We will report such activities and user details to the appropriate law enforcement agencies and the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).

## 3. Adult Content & Nudity
*   Public profile photos must not contain nudity or sexually explicit content.
*   Sending unsolicited sexually explicit images (cyber-flashing) is strictly prohibited and will result in account termination.

## 4. Fake Profiles & Impersonation
*   Users must use their real photos and represent themselves accurately.
*   Impersonating others, creating fake profiles, or catfishing is not allowed.

## 5. Reporting & Blocking
We empower our users to maintain their safety:
*   **Blocking:** You can block any user you do not wish to interact with.
*   **Reporting:** If you encounter behavior that violates these guidelines, please use the in-app "Report" feature or contact us directly. All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

## 6. Data Safety
We are committed to protecting your personal data. Please refer to our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) to understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.
